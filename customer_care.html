Frequently Asked Questions

Granville Homes is available Monday-Friday from 7 a.m. to 6 p.m., and is oncall for emergencies after hours.

If you have a non-emergency issue, please contact (559) 268-2000 and leave a message with the Customer Care answering service. They will send a notification to the Customer Care team to contact you the following business day.

The best way to report non-emergency issues is to submit an online service request. Your request will be sent directly to the Customer Care department where a representative will contact you within 24 hours.

While issues are inconvenient, not every issue is an emergency. These are the condidtions our trade partners respond to as emergencies:

  • Total loss of electricity
  • Total loss of water
  • Plumbing leak that requires the entire water supply to be shut off to the house
  • Gas leak
  • Total loss of air conditioning when the outside temperature is above 100F
  • Total loos of heat when the outside temperature is below 50F

If you have an emergency during business hours, please contact the Customer Care department at (559) 268-2000 to speak with the first available representative. If you have an emergency after hours, please contact the Customer Care department at (559) 268-2000 to speak with the Customer Care answering service. They will report your emergency to the on-call Customer Care representative who will call you back within a few minutes of receiving the call.

Customer Care technicians are available for assessment appointments Monday through Saturday as early as 8 a.m. and as late as 4 p.m.

Each trade partner who builds and performs warranty work on your home has their own business hours of operation. Most of our trade partners are available for non-emergency issues in your home Monday-Friday from 8 a.m. until 3 p.m. For emergencies after hours, please contact (559) 268-2000 to be connected with an on-call Customer Care representative.

The Customer Care department has developed a maintenance program called the Granville Care Package for our homeowners. Our pricing is very competitive, as we have negotiated prices with our trade partners. For more information about the Granville Care package, please visit our website at www.gvhomes.com

The warranty period of a Granville home begins at the close of escrow. Since your home is comprised of many components and was built by multiple trade partners, the warranty on each component varies. Please refer to the Homeowner Guide you received at the signing of your contract. For specific details, please contact the Customer Care Department.
