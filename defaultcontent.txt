    <h1>Frequently Asked Questions</h1>
    <button class="accordion">What are the customer care hours?</button>
    <div class="panel">
        <p>Granville Homes is available Monday-Friday from 7 a.m. to 6 p.m., and is oncall for emergencies after hours.</p>
    </div>

    <button class="accordion">What to do if i need help after hours?</button>
    <div class="panel">
        <p>If you have a non-emergency issue, please contact <strong>(559) 268-2000</strong> and leave a message with the Customer Care answering service. They will send a notification to the Customer Care team to contact you the following business day.</p>
    </div>

    <button class="accordion">How do i report a non-emergency issue?</button>
    <div class="panel">
        <p>The best way to report non-emergency issues is to submit an <a href="http://www.gvhomes.com/service-request-form"></a>online service request</a>. Your request will be sent directly to the Customer Care department where a representative will contact you within 24 hours.</p>
    </div>

    <button class="accordion">What constitutes as an emergency?</button>
    <div class="panel">
        <p>While issues are inconvenient, not every issue is an emergency. These are the condidtions our trade partners respond to as emergencies:</p>
        <ul>
            <li>Total loss of electricity</li>
            <li>Total loss of water</li>
            <li>Plumbing leak that requires the entire water supply to be shut off to the house</li>
            <li>Gas leak</li>
            <li>Total loss of air conditioning when the outside temperature is above 100F</li>
            <li>Total loos of heat when the outside temperature is below 50F</li>
        </ul>
    </div>

    <button class="accordion">How do I report an emergency issue?</button>
    <div class="panel">
        <p>If you have an emergency during business hours, please contact the Customer Care department at <strong>(559) 268-2000</strong> to speak with the first available representative. If you have an emergency after hours, please contact the Customer Care department at <strong>(559) 268-2000</strong> to speak with the Customer Care answering service. They will report your emergency to the on-call Customer Care representative who will call you back within a few minutes of receiving the call.</p>
    </div>

    <button class="accordion">When are customer care technicians available?</button>
    <div class="panel">
        <p>Customer Care technicians are available for assessment appointments Monday through Saturday as early as 8 a.m. and as late as 4 p.m.</p>
    </div>

    <button class="accordion">Can I schedule work over the weekend?</button>
    <div class="panel">
        <p>Each trade partner who builds and performs warranty work on your home has their own business hours of operation. Most of our trade partners are available for non-emergency issues in your home Monday-Friday from 8 a.m. until 3 p.m. For emergencies after hours, please contact <strong>(559) 268-2000</strong> to be connected with an on-call Customer Care representative.</p>
    </div>

    <button class="accordion">Does Granville Homes do maintenance work?</button>
    <div class="panel">
        <p>The Customer Care department has developed a maintenance program called the Granville Care Package for our homeowners. Our pricing is very competitive, as we have negotiated prices with our trade partners. For more information about the Granville Care package, please visit our website at www.gvhomes.com</p>
    </div>

    <button class="accordion">What is the warranty period?</button>
    <div class="panel">
        <p>The warranty period of a Granville home begins at the close of escrow. Since your home is comprised of many components and was built by multiple trade partners, the warranty on each component varies. Please refer to the Homeowner Guide you received at the signing of your contract. For specific details, please contact the Customer Care Department.</p>
    </div>
