FresGO! App

FresGO! is a free, easy to use, real-time app transforming how Fresno solves issues. Report infrastructure and non-emergency public safety issues such as graffiti, broken sprinklers, potholes and more from your smartphone or computer. This multi-lingual application automatically routs the report to the proper department and notifies you as soon as the problem is resolved!
